Astronomy

The universe has fascinated me since childhood. I enjoy stargazing with a telescope and like looking at photographs of astronomical objects. Astronomy is a fascinating science. We know so much about the universe just from observation. Astronomy is one of the few remaining fields of science where amateurs can make meaningful contributions towards the advancement of our knowledge.

In the past, I had taken part in the Astronomy olympiad and attended the Orientation-cum-Selection Camp. At IIT Bombay, I actively take part in the activities of the Astronomy Club, Krittika.

I have observed many celestial objects with telescopes. The list includes the sun, the moon, various planets and stars, as well as many star clusters, nebulae and galaxies. I have also performed observations at various dark sky sites.
